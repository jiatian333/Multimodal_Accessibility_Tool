Welcome to 15-min city

This web application visualizes the travel time from various locations to public transport stations. The initial page displays a static isochrone map, illustrating travel times from all locations in the city of Zurich to their nearest public transport station. If you wish to view a detailed isochrone map around a specific station, please enter the station's name.

Last-Mile Connectivity Analysis of Zurich

The map visualizes travel times from various locations to their nearest public transport station – so called isochrones, helping planners easily identify areas where additional stations might be needed.

If you wish to check the isochrone map around a specific station, please switch to the dynamic version.

Now you are at the dynamic version of isochrones. Please enter a station name to see its surrounding isochrones.
