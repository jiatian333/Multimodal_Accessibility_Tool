Welcome to 15-min city

Here you can see a static isochrone map for a selected mode of transport. The map visualizes travel times from different locations to their nearest station, helping planners easily identify areas where additional stations might be needed.

If you wish to check the travel time from a specific station to surrounding locations, please enter the station name below.
