Welcome to 15-min city

This web application visualizes the travel time from various locations to public transport stations. The initial page displays a static isochrone map, illustrating travel times from all locations in the city of Zurich to their nearest public transport station. If you wish to view a detailed isochrone map around a specific station, please enter the station's name.
